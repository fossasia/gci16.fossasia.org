GCI – Why FOSSASIA?

This year at CodeIn, I’ve helped FOSSASIA in many projects as part of CodeIn tasks. But, why FOSSASIA? There are lots of organizations with lots of interesting projects, and my first task was for Drupal. Then, why?

Well, I firstly did the tasks I found interesting (or easy), but then I decided I should stick to an organization. I had a look at all the organizations and found 3 options: Drupal, FOSSASIA and SCoRe.

As Drupal only had one project (Drupal) and the projects at SCoRe weren’t interesting for me, I started making FOSSASIA tasks.

After making a few tasks, the FOSSASIA GCI16 website got my attention, and I started to make all the tasks related to the website. Also, I started helping students out of CodeIn. So, when I saw that they were searching people for helping, I applied.

To be a mantainer is awesome. I’ve helped lots of people, implemented new useful things (like Danger) and learned to work with a team. 

I feel like FOSSASIA was the rigth choice, and I hope I can continue helping, even after CodeIn and CodeHeat.

Before finishing, I’d like to thank FOSSASIA, the mentors team, my partners at the mantainers team and specifically, to @robbyoconnor and @jigyasia for his initial support, accepting me on the mantainers team and fixing my mistakes (mostly with TravisCI).

Thank you!
