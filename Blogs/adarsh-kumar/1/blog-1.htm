Participated in GCI…

December 1, 2016 idealadarsh

Just completed my first task in Google code-in.

I got an email from a social site titled “Registration started for Google code-in”, I read about that further on google’s website and registered there.

This is a very vast community with different people working on projects. This is ( totally new  and ) an awesome experience for me in this area.

I am currently working with Fossasia, it’s an organisation contributing in Google Code-In.

Check their students website also gci15.fossasia.org
