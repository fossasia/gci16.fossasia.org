Why I participated in Google Code In 2016

Posted on December 10, 2016January 7, 2017 by zehatablog

 

I’m Zenon, 15. I participated in Google Code In for several years because I love to contribute to open source companies to make it better. I feel that my works are more significant compared to companies where there may be thousands of full-time employees and my works would be buried deep within the millions of commits the rest have made. I hope to be able to learn more about coding in this year’s GCI and more about Github. FOSSASIA is a really good organisation for me to contribute to because I get to start with easier tasks before I do the more challenging ones.
