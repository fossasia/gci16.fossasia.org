My connection to FOSSASIA and GCI

Posted on December 10, 2016 by prabh29

Greetings everyone, My name is Prabhdeep Singh and I am from India, Punjab. I am currently 14 years old and I hope to be a programmer. Fossasia has helped me understand certain things like the Github Repositories which were quite hard to understand at first. By participating in the Google Code-In I Can get more opportunities for myself in life. Here is a Photo of mine –

Every Mentor and Student at Fossasia are most likely really helpful and the mentors are very good and I don’t think they need any sort of improvement as they are the best as I know it. The Fossasia community can help me to understand more about certain programming languages and teach me the most about Github which is really helpful. I am participating in Google Code-In only to test my skills and see where I stand so that I can improve myself even more and perform much better the next year. You can view the Fossasia website here and the Google Code-In Website here. You can also visit the Fossasia blogs section here. We must use our skills and whatever we are good in for the betterment of our future, which I am trying to do.
