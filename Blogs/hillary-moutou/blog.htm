Google-code in (GCI)

Posted on December 8, 2016 by hillamu2
Reply

I attended a youth forum at the annual InfoTech held in Mauritius last week and someone spoke about Google Code-In (GCI) and I immediately became fascinated with it. As soon as I got home, I decided to do some research about it and found it extremely captivating so I talked about it with my parents and friends. In the technology-dominated world we are living in I find it important to being able to actually build something fast or watching other passionate programmers build with enthusiasm and that motivated me to register on GCI. What I find really interesting and motivating with GCI is that it also allows users to choose non-coding tasks ; it thus caters with the skills of all users.  GCI also includes supports by an international community of volunteer mentors to help us learn, discover and ameliorate our skills.I strongly encourage anyone reading this to participate in GCI since it will help you if you want a future in IT (visit the page at http://gci16.fossasia.org).

Experience joining FOSSASIA

Posted on December 7, 2016 by hillamu2
Reply

Hello! I recently joined FOSSASIA and I must say that I’m really happy I did. For those to which FOSSASIA is still unknown I’m going to give you a brief introduction . FOSSASIA is a non-profit organization supporting developers and makers of Free and Open Source technologies and has the aim of developping and adapting open technologies for social change with a focus on Asian users and I strongly advised you to visit its page. (link is http://fossasia.org).

I was pretty shocked that FOSSASIA offers resources to students, developers and start-ups for them to develop their projects. I was shocked since FOSSASIA helps others without expecting nothing in return which is pretty rare in this world and I’m glad that non-profit organization like this still exists. What I really appreciate about FOSSASIA is that it doesn’t under-estimate the capacity of young students and give them a unique opportunity to prove their worths . Since joining FOSSASIA,  I strongly believe in creating a better future and using IT in the process.
